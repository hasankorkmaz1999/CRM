Help

Welcome to the help page for CRM System, your guide to managing users, customers, and events efficiently. Here, you'll find an overview of what this CRM can do, how it benefits you, and how to use it.

What is the CRM System?

The CRM System is designed to help businesses and teams manage their customer relationships, track events, and organize communication in one place.

With our CRM, you can:

  • Manage users and roles
  • Track customer data and purchase history
  • Schedule and manage events
  • View sales analytics and insights
  • Organize internal discussions and threads

How to use it

Follow these steps to get started with the CRM:

1. Logging in

Enter your credentials on the login page. If you don't have an account, contact an administrator. Alternatively, you can log in as a guest to explore the system.

2. Managing Users

Go to the Users section to add, edit, or delete users. Assign roles and permissions to control access. View the number of sales each user has recorded directly in their profile.

3. Managing Customers

Navigate to the Customers tab to view detailed customer profiles, contact information, and complete purchase history. Each customer profile allows you to track individual spending habits and manage interactions effectively.

5. Creating Events

Use the Calendar section to schedule meetings, webinars, or other business-related events.

6. Discussion Threads

Use the InfoDesk feature to communicate with team members, share announcements and resolve customer inquiries.

7. Stay Organized with Your To-Do List

Use the To-Do List to stay organized, prioritize tasks, and track your progress efficiently.

I hope this guide helps you navigate the CRM system with ease. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out. Enjoy using your CRM!"
